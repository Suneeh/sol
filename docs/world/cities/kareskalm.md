# Kareskalm

## Lore
A Bavarian boy wandered from the misty foothills into the quiet streets of a town and, charmed by its gentle rivers, sleepy towers and people obsessed with nature, whispered a new name that fit his heart: Kareskalm and the city answered with a hush of bells. That night, under lantern-light, the cobblestones hummed with old stories, and Lukas felt the city breathe a little softer, as if accepting its new name and the small, brave magic of a child who loved it.
